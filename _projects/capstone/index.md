title: Senior Capstone - AutoRotating First-Aid Delivery Device
description: >-
  Designed and prototyped a novel autorotating wing system to improve the accuracy and reliability of humanitarian aid airdrops, providing controlled, parachute-free descent for first-aid payloads. The design applied biomimetic principles inspired by the aerodynamics of maple seeds (samaras) to achieve stable autorotation. The project spanned the full engineering design cycle: aerodynamic concept development, CFD simulation to characterize descent and rotational behavior, fabrication of a carbon fiber prototype, and experimental validation through small-scale drop testing, which confirmed steady, repeatable autorotation.
